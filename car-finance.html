FAQ

Quick answers

Will checking my rate impact my credit score?
No. We use soft pre-assessments to compare lenders before any formal credit check. Your file isn't touched until you've reviewed your options and given consent to proceed.
How fast can I get approved?
Most straightforward pre-approvals come back within 1 business hour. More complex scenarios (self-employed, past credit issues, unusual assets) are typically approved within 24 hours.
Can I get pre-approved before choosing a car?
Yes — and we strongly recommend it. Pre-approvals are valid for 90 days, lock in your borrowing power, and give you serious negotiating leverage at dealerships and private sales.
Can you beat dealership finance?
Almost always. Dealers push one or two lenders that pay them the highest commission. We compare 63+ banks and non-banks for the best rate, structure and lender for your situation.
Do you finance private sales and interstate?
Yes. We regularly settle private sales, auction purchases and interstate deals — handling PPSR checks, encumbrance clearance and direct payment to the seller.
What if I'm self-employed?
We're specialists. Low-doc loans (from 6 months ABN), new ABN options, GST-registered and non-registered, sole trader or company — see our business finance page for more.
Is there a fee for your service?
Our pre-approval process is cost-free and commitment-free — there's nothing to pay to see your options. If you choose to proceed, an origination fee applies; it's only ever payable from the proceeds of your loan contract, so nothing comes out of your pocket. Because every lender's pricing is slightly different, we'll walk you through all fees upfront once your pre-approval is in place — no surprises.
